## Video candidate pass

**Status: Not recommended (no strong candidate).**

- **Criterion check:** A video is flagged only when a transition mechanism is the learning target, when there are ≥3 sequential causal stages, when an essential cycle is present, or when a sub-observable transformation must be shown. Chapter 13 is organized as a static taxonomy (four parallel tracks) plus a static artifact (the card) plus a static placement (the ladder). Its content is comparison and hierarchy, not sequence or transformation.
- **Reason:** The one process-like element — "naive estimate → matched re-estimate → reported gap" inside project A1 — is a two-step analytical contrast, not a ≥3-stage causal mechanism, and it is better served by the static failure-mode matrix (Figure 4) and by Chapter 14's pipeline. The Evidence-Ladder firewall is a boundary, not a transition the reader needs to watch happen.
- **Suggested format (only if a video is mandated elsewhere):** a short build-on reveal of Figure 1 — rungs appear, then each track span is placed, then the firewall line drops in above L3 — to dramatize that nothing reaches the proprietary region. This is a presentation aid, not a learning-target transition; recommend keeping it static. **Recommend: no video for this chapter; reserve the video budget for Chapter 14's pipeline.**
