## Cross-cutting flags for the author / accuracy pass

- **All script-lift / ROI figures (4–44%, 20:1) are vendor-generated and attribution-circular** — never
  cite as independent evidence (Ch 1, 4, 5).
- **Channel-growth and "AI reduces MLR 50–65%" / "95% of AI pilots fail" stats are industry/un-refereed
  and sometimes conflicting** — attribute, present both sides, `[verify]`.
- **Three book-position constructs must stay flagged, not asserted** (per the firewall): susceptibility-proxy
  features (Ch 3), brand-equity-vs-clinical-value correlation (Ch 2/9), genuine-MoE-vs-relabel (Ch 7).
- **The Evidence Ladder (0–6)** is the book's own construct (TRL × OCEBM adaptation) — present as such.
- **BLOCKER (TIKTOC Risk 1 / Open Q1):** partner-collaboration framing + Stage-2 proprietary-replication
  scope unresolved — surfaces in Ch 13 and Ch 14 notes.
- **Data limits:** Medicare Part D does not directly observe NBRx / new starts (must proxy); Medicare/Medicaid
  population skew limits generalization to commercially-insured segments where co-pay coupons operate.
